JuiceControl

overview information architecture consumption screen device control screen iphone app

JuiceControl is an attempt to save the world by giving you more control over your energy and a better idea of your consumption.

Saving Energy is one of the most important topics in nowadays, not just because of the effects our energy consumption has on the planet and environment, but also because all the fossil fuels are coming to an end.

Something needs to be done and as always it has to start from the people.

By using JuiceControl it is easy monitor your own energy & water consumption, so you can reduce it and optimize your household technology.

Consumption screen

section 003 /

The consumption screen is the most important part of the app showing you a detailed analysis of your usage and how the usage develops over time.

The most important goal of JuiceControl is to empower you to analyze and reduce your consumption of precious resources like water and energy.

To make this possible for everybody JuiceControl provides information on multiple levels, starting with a broad comparison to tell you if you are using more or less than last month.

If you are interested you can review more in depth information like the usage for every device.

JuiceControl provides an additional section with a calculated CO2 footprint for your resource consumption. You can get a more realistic footprint by adding other information like the amount of driving you do per month.

Device Control

section 004 /

The device control screen list all connected devices within your home, organized into custom groups like kitchen and living room.

You can easily turn off a whole group with the switch next to the groups name. JuiceControl acts intelligently, so that machines which are by you or the manufacture set to a "stay on" mode, will never be turned off. This way your fridge keeps cooling.

Within the device screen you can also switch individual devices on and off, this way you iPad can function as a remote control for your dishwasher, which you can start once everybody left the house.

Additionally every device shows overall running costs as a total as well as the used resources. This way you know which device you should replace to save resources and money.

Turn off your device when you are not at home to save energy.

JuiceControl iPhone app

section 005 /

The iPhone app is a special use case because it is mostly used to get a quick overview of energy costs or to turn devices on/off.

The functionality is reduced in comparison to the iPad app, to make using JuiceControl on the iPhone easy and fun.
