Diversity Squared

overview information architecture name & design

Diversity Squared (D2) is a quarterly published iPad magazine about Berlin featuring articles on culture, food, scene and discovering the city.

Navigate the magazine by swiping, the arrows show you in which direction to scroll.

D2 is a digital magazine mostly intended for people who recently moved to Berlin. However, due to the ever changing nature of Berlin the magazine is interesting for people who live in berlin all their live as well.

The content is created by a team of editors and enhanced by comments of the community, displayed next to each article with an easy way to interact.

Information Architecture

section 002 /

Four topics are reviewed by a team of editors every three month, with a focus on one of each every time.

For a magazine which features mostly content that is unknown to the audience we decided to use images for the table of content, reflecting the length of the stories content.

The two main stories are displayed at the top. Below the user finds four stories with related topics. All those stories are part of the main topic of this issue.

The images at the bottom stand for the remaining three out of the four topics and magazine related information like authors.

Within the those three topics there is no big story but rather some small articles and updating information about concerts, restaurants and other tips.

Name & Design

section 003 /

D2 short for Diversity Squared originates from the idea of Berlin being a city that stands for diversity. One could even argue that diversity makes Berlin what it is, and thus should be treasured.

The layout is intended to stay unnoticed thus leaving the content to create the atmosphere of every issue.

The reader can easily hide text to get a fullscreen view of images and videos. Further more the opinion of the community is integrated deeply into the magazine by giving the reader the possibility to view comments for every article by dragging the comment bar in from the right side. It is also easy to post your own comment here.
