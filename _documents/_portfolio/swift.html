key values logo artwork colors print materials digital fleet & architecture

Swift is a case study for an airline corporate identity which focuses on speed, comfort and trust.

key values

section 001 /

comfort means the possibility to let go of the stress of everyday working life. It means enjoying the time you spent on the aircraft and to arrive relaxed and ready to start your day at your destination.

trust in your airline is essential to enjoy your flight. You should not have to worry about delays, loss of luggage or security issues. Once you arrive at the airport we will manage your luggage, transfers and make sure you do not miss your flight, while you take a well deserved break.

speed is what differentiates swift from other airlines. From booking to luggage pickup everything is optimized for speed. The airplane arrives at the airport before you do and will be ready for takeoff when you are. Spent more time at your destination and less time on the airport.

Logo

section 002 /

Speed is the most important attribute of swift. This is why speed is present throughout the entire corporate design, including the company name and the logo.

Swift [swɪft] – happening quickly or promptly, moving or capable of moving at high speed.

The logo is tilted towards the right, reflecting a forwards movement.

Corporate Colors

section 003 /

A good color balance is essential to create the right feeling with the used colors. Using mostly yellow emphasises the speed of swift, with a friendly orange and a calm green to show the relaxed feeling experienced when traveling with swift.

Yellow is a warm color which stands for communication, joviality and understanding, but in this tone it also gives a feeling of the speed swift stands for.

Orange is a warm color like yellow and shares much of its meaning. It stands for energy and liveliness as well as relaxation. Combined with yellow it gives the swift corporate identity its friendly and open character.

Green stands for regeneration and recovery. Security, stability, confidence, hope and harmony. The darker tone of green produces a secure and regenerative atmosphere.

Shades of grey provide a good contrast for text while they harmonize better with other colors thank black does.

Print materials

section 004 /

The corporate yellow is used for print materials like boarding passes and letters so they are easily associated with swift.

Digital materials

section 005 /

Speed is also present in the digital products, especially in the usability and the workflow of the applications.

A fast user experience is created by focusing on the essential and providing easy access to common tasks like booking or checking flight dates.

The swift mobile app offers fast access to your customer data and boarding pass. It also provides convenient functions like temperature and weather for the current location as well as the destination.

Corporate fleet & corporate architecture

section 006 /

The corporate values of swift are present in the fleet as well as in the architecture.

The vehicles and planes are designed to look fast by using mostly yellow and the corporate pattern.

The bright yellow combined with green gives swift a prominent presence on any airport.

The architectural design supports the main function of an area. Areas like the check-in counter are mostly yellow, emphasizing the speed and making it easy to find on the airport. Areas in which customers may spent more time, like the interior of an airplane or the lounge, are mostly dark green which gives a feeling of calmness and relaxation.
